Part 2

What OSCortex Does — Full Capabilities

Written so a 55-year-old hotel owner or workwear company director can follow every word.

💬

Communications — Your Messages, Handled

OSCortex manages your business communications across every channel, simultaneously.

  • WhatsApp — Reads incoming messages, responds in your voice, routes urgent ones to you, sends follow-ups, and even sends voice notes. Your prospects and customers get fast, thoughtful replies — whether you're in a meeting, on site, or asleep.
  • Email — Handles your inbox end to end. Drafts replies, organises messages by priority, follows up on unanswered threads, and manages the back-and-forth that eats your mornings. Works with Gmail and any standard email setup.
  • Telegram — Runs full channels with buttons, polls, media, and automated message flows. Ideal for membership communities, internal teams, or client groups.
  • iMessage & SMS — Reads and responds to text messages. No channel left unattended.
  • Multi-channel at once — A prospect WhatsApps you, emails your office, and messages on Telegram? OSCortex sees it all, keeps the context joined up, and responds appropriately on each channel. No crossed wires. No dropped threads.
🔍

Research & Intelligence — Know Before You Go

OSCortex does your homework before you've had your coffee.

  • Prospect research — Before a call, OSCortex pulls together everything public about the company and the person you're meeting: company size, recent news, decision-maker background, social media activity. You walk in prepared.
  • Competitor intelligence — Full public-information profiles on any competitor. What they're saying, what they're charging, where they're showing up, what their customers are saying about them.
  • Live web search — Current events, market data, news, pricing — searched and summarised in seconds.
  • Social media monitoring — Tracks topics, trends, and mentions on X/Twitter in real time.
  • Blog and news monitoring — Watches any website, blog, or news source you care about.
  • Video and podcast summaries — Point it at a YouTube video or podcast episode. It watches, reads the transcript, and delivers a structured brief. A 90-minute interview becomes a two-minute read.
  • Document analysis — Hand it a PDF, article, or report. It reads the whole thing and gives you a structured breakdown in seconds.
✍️

Content & Copy — Created, Not Templated

OSCortex writes original content matched to your business, your voice, and your audience.

  • Sales emails and sequences — First-touch emails, follow-up sequences, objection-handling variations, re-engagement campaigns.
  • Landing pages and ad copy — Long form, short form, matched to the audience you're targeting.
  • Social media content — LinkedIn posts, X threads, platform-native formats that don't sound like they came from a robot.
  • Proposals and quotes — Structured, professional proposals using your pricing framework and the prospect's specific situation.
  • Scripts — Video scripts, phone scripts, discovery call guides. Word-for-word material your team can use today.
  • Visual content — AI-generated marketing images, social graphics, concept visuals, diagrams, flowcharts, promotional video clips, and audio jingles.
⚙️

Operations — The Admin You Hate, Done

  • Task and project tracking — What's open, what's overdue, what's coming up. Nothing falls through the cracks.
  • Morning briefings — Every day before you start, a clean summary: today's priorities, overdue items, upcoming deadlines. Three to five bullet points. Read it with your coffee.
  • Lead management — Every prospect captured, profiled, and tracked with full context.
  • Pipeline management — Where every deal sits, what happened last, what needs to happen next.
  • Meeting prep — Before any call or meeting, OSCortex generates a brief: who you're meeting, their background, your history, what to raise.
  • Follow-up automation — Scheduled messages and check-ins that go out automatically.
🤖

Automation — Set It, Trust It

  • Scheduled tasks — Daily, weekly, or custom-timed work that runs without you touching anything.
  • Multi-step sequences — Trigger happens → OSCortex takes action → checks the response → takes the next action → logs it.
  • Background processing — Give it a complex job at 5pm. Results are waiting when you log on at 8am.
  • Parallel work — Multiple AI agents working on different parts of a project simultaneously.
  • Browser automation — Fills forms, extracts data from websites, monitors pages for changes.
📊

Data & Analysis — Numbers Into Answers

  • Spreadsheet analysis — Drop in a CSV or spreadsheet. OSCortex reads it and tells you what matters: trends, outliers, patterns, recommendations.
  • Financial tracking — Revenue trends, cost patterns, margin analysis.
  • Performance reporting — Raw numbers turned into clear reports with actual insight, not just charts.
  • Custom calculations — Complex modelling, forecasting, scenario analysis. It runs real code to get real answers.

The Compounding Value Story

Designed to be delivered as a narrative — in a meeting, on a call, or in a follow-up email. Learn it. Own it.

The Flywheel

Most business tools are flat. You pay for them in January, and in December they do exactly the same thing. Same features. Same limitations. Same value. OSCortex is different. It compounds.

Month 1

OSCortex is learning your business. Your products, services, pricing, clients, preferences, voice. It's useful immediately — handling communications, creating content, running research — but it's still new. You're teaching it.

Month 3

It knows your top clients by name. It knows your pricing tiers without asking. It remembers that you prefer proposals formatted a certain way. It recalls that a particular customer always pays late and adjusts the follow-up tone accordingly. It's not just doing tasks — it's doing them the way you would.

Month 6

It knows your business. Not in a general sense. Specifically. It knows your seasonal patterns, your common objections, your best-performing content, your biggest competitors. You stop giving it context. It already has the context.

Month 12

It knows your business better than most of your staff. Your average employee remembers what happened last week. OSCortex has a perfect record of every conversation, every decision, every client interaction since day one — and it can recall any of it in seconds.

Month 24

It's irreplaceable. Switching to something else doesn't just mean changing tools. It means losing all of that accumulated intelligence. Starting from zero.

"Every month you run OSCortex, it becomes more valuable. Not because we add features — because it adds knowledge. YOUR knowledge. About YOUR business. The system you have at twelve months is worth dramatically more than the system you had at three months."

"That's why we don't call it a subscription. It's an investment. The value doesn't stay flat — it grows. And after a year, the cost of NOT running it is far higher than the cost of running it, because walking away means losing everything it's learned."

Part 7

Words to Kill

These are banned. Every one of them costs you credibility.

Word / PhraseWhy It's Banned
"Revolutionary"Empty hype. Everyone claims it. Nobody believes it. Makes you sound like every other AI pitch.
"Game-changing"Overused to the point of meaninglessness. Same problem.
"AI-powered" (standalone)Vague. Everything claims to be "AI-powered" now. Always say what it does, specifically.
"Cutting-edge"Triggers scepticism. Sounds like you're selling technology, not results. They want things done.
"Seamlessly"Nobody believes this word. It sounds like marketing copy. Drop it.
"Synergy"Corporate jargon. Instant credibility loss with the SME owners you're selling to.
"Leverage" (as a verb)Boardroom language that makes real people's eyes glaze over.
"Bot" / "Chatbot"Immediately makes people think of those terrible website chat widgets. Kill this word on sight.
"Automate your business"Scares people. It sounds like you're removing them from their own company.
"Disrupt"Silicon Valley language. Your prospects in Sheffield and Birmingham don't care about disruption.
"Set and forget"Implies no oversight. Say "the system runs it — you review it" instead.
"Platform"Vague SaaS word. OSCortex isn't a platform they log into. It's a deployment inside their business.
"Solution"The most overused word in B2B. Replace with what it actually does.
"Onboarding" (with clients)Use "getting you live" or "getting you set up." Onboarding sounds like SaaS. This is deployment.
